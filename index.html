OUR FLAVORS

Smiles Made Fresh Daily!
Happy Day Dessert Factory is located on the North Side of Pittsburgh. Our menu includes:

  1. 50+ flavors of Premium Homemade Hard Ice Cream
  2. 13 flavors of Water Ice
  3. Frozen Soft Custard
  4. Frozen Yogurt
  5. Almond Milk Soft Serve

Obviously we have your classic favorites of Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, etc... every day,
but check out our menu for some of our lesser known flavors that are sure to become your new favorite.
